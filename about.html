About Us

PromptX AI is an application developed by Xenojit India. The purpose of the app is to provide a collection of useful AI prompts for creativity, productivity, and learning.

PromptX AI helps users discover prompts for various purposes such as content creation, social media ideas, writing assistance, and AI tools usage. Our goal is to make AI prompts easily accessible and organized in one place.

The application is designed with a simple and fast user experience so that users can quickly browse, save, and explore prompts without needing to create an account.

PromptX AI may include advertisements to support development and keep the application free for users.
